Buffalo

The American Bison is known are the American Buffalo. It is a North America species and became nearly extinct in the 19th century. The male is slightly larger than the female and considerably heavier. A bison buffalo has got unkempt, dark brown, long winter coat. Their weight can range from 318 to 907 kg (701 to 2,000 lb).
