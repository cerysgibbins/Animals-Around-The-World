Panda

The Panda is a traditional Chinese black and white giant panda and are native to the south central China. The reason why the name ‘giant panda’ is used is because it differentiates it from the red Panda. If a Panda is living in the wild they tend to live on grass, meat or rodents. If living in captivity they may eat honey, fish, eggs and oranges along with specially prepared food.[4]
