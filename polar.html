Polar Bear

A Polar Bear is a meat-eating bear whose natural range lies largely within the Arctic Circle, surrounding the Arctic Ocean with its surround seas and land masses. A male bear is known as a boar and a female bear is a sow. A boar weighs around 350 – 700 kg (770 – 1,540 lb) [1]. While a sow is about half the size.
